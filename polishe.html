Landfill Monitoring Dashboard

As part of my MSc assignment at UCL, my team and I created a Landfill Monitoring Dashboard with a special focus on DKI Jakarta and the Bantar Gebang Landfill. The project aimed to fill the existing gaps in landfill monitoring technologies for these specific areas. Working as consultants, we employed remote sensing technologies to address pressing urban and environmental issues. The project was well-received and earned an 'A' grade. What sets this project apart is our use of Xaringan, an R package, for creating our presentation slides. For a more detailed exploration, feel free to visit the project here.
